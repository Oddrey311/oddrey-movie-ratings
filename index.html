The Person Behind the Ratings

Welcome! I'm a massive cinema enthusiast who believes every movie deserves a fair shake. This archive is my personal journey through the big screen, tracking everything I've watched at the cinema since 2014.

My Rating System

I use a weighted 100-point system to keep things objective, but "Enjoyment" always plays a role. Here is how the colors break down:

95-100: Masterpiece. Life-changing cinema.
90-94: Elite. Incredible execution.
80-89: Great. Highly recommended.
70-79: Good. Worth a watch.
60-69: Decent. Has some flaws.
40-59: Mediocre. Struggled to engage.
20-39: Poor. Disappointing.
0-19: Abyssal. Hard to sit through.
